Frequently Asked Questions

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all electronic items, provided they are in their original packaging and condition. For defective products, we offer a free exchange or full refund within 90 days.

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days within North America. Express shipping options (1-2 business days) are available at checkout for an additional fee.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we ship to over 50 countries worldwide. Shipping fees and delivery times vary by destination and will be calculated at the checkout page.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Visa, MasterCard, American Express, PayPal, and Google Pay. All transactions are securely processed.

How do I track my order?

Once your order ships, you will receive an email confirmation containing a tracking number and a link to the carrier's website.
